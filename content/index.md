Welcome to my bliki — a personal blog/wiki hybrid where I think out loud,
gather notes, and publish from my own corner of the web.

This site follows the [POSSE](https://indieweb.org/POSSE) principle:
**P**ublish (on your) **O**wn **S**ite, **S**yndicate **E**lsewhere.

Some pages here are short and unfinished. That's the point of a
[bliki](https://martinfowler.com/bliki/WhatIsaBliki.html) — notes evolve.
